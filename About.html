About Me

Hi, I'm Khadijah Kolapo, a passionate UI/UX designer with a love for creating intuitive, user-centered designs that bridge functionality with aesthetic appeal. My journey in design began with a curiosity for how best people interact with digital products, which quickly turned into a deep commitment to crafting desihns that improves the overall experiences of users.

I am proficient with the use of Figma, html, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, and Word press.

With a background in Occupational Therapy, I bring a unique perspective to the design process. Whether it's wireframing, prototyping, or conducting user research, I thrive on the details that make an interface not only look good but work seamlessly.

When I'm not designing, you'll likely find me eating good food, reading, seeing talkshows, documentaries, or a really good movie..
